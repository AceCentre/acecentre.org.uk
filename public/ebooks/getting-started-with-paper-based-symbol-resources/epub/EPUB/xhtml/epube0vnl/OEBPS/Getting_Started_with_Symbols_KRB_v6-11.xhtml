Chapter 1 – An Introduction to AAC

 

This is Ria at 5 years old. She has physical difficulties, some visual difficulties and is not able to use speech to communicate. Ria uses a mixture of paper-based and electronic AAC to support her communication.

Here’s Ria using a symbol based communication book to express that she wants Karen to blow her a “big” bubble!

﻿Ria

When Ria started school she had her own symbol-based communication book that she took with her. Of course, Ria could use her book to ask for things – but she could also do so much more than this. Using the symbols in her book, she could have a chat with her teacher and tell her mum about her school day. Ria really does have a lot to say!
