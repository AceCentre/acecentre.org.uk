AAC can be used to help people understand what is said to them, to help people express themselves, or both.

AAC is often divided into Aided and Unaided categories. Aided AAC describes any type of AAC that uses some sort of equipment, be it a pencil or a computer. Unaided AAC is communication that you do using your body with no other equipment.

Unaided AAC

Unaided AAC includes gesture and as well as more formal signing systems and languages.
