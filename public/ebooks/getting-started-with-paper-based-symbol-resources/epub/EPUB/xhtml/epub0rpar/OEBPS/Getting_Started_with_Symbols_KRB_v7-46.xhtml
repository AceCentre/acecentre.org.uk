For example, you could say “Look!, [there’s] Peppa” or “Look, Mummy Pig.” If something has gone wrong you could say, “Uh oh!”. In many episodes you might find yourself commenting, “Daddy Pig [is] funny” or “funny Peppa”. You could notice that “Mummy Pig [is] happy” or that “George [is] sad”. Perhaps you, “don’t like George [being] sad”. A favourite character could easily be added onto the chart. It would then be a great opportunity to spot the character during any episode – “Look! Danny Dog” (or whoever the favourite is!).

The same approach could be taken with a wide range of television programs from soap operas to talent style shows like Strictly Come Dancing.
