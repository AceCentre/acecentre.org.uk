Chapter 4 – Ideas and Resources for Getting Going

With this chart you could agree to “sing along” to a track. You could comment that the music is “loud” or direct it to be “more loud”! You could comment that you “like” the song or the singing, or request that someone “stop sing along”. You could agree to listen to a “different song” or to listen to the same song “again”.

Listen to Music

This chart is also for talking about music but is a little more complicated and contains the Core 15 chart symbols.

Again there are lots of ideas that can be communicated with just this vocabulary. For example, you could comment that the music is “happy” or “sad”, or say that it’s “my favourite”. You could say you have a “question” – what is “this music”? You could agree to “dance” or perhaps direct “you help me dance”. You might comment that it is a “different playlist” or say that “I [do] not like [the] playlist”.
