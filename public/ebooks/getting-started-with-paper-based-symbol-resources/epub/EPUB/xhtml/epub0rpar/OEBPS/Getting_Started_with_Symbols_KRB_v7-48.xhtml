Beauty

Another motivating activity for some may be activities around beauty such as putting on make up or painting nails. For some, these sort of charts might constitute part of a daily routine.

This chart (also shown above in the dressing up section) can be used to discuss hair and make up.

Make Up Chart

There is so much you can say with this chart. You could agree to “brush hair” or “help brush hair”, then “put on [a] hair clip”. You might comment that “wow, your hair clip [is] great”. You might agree to “look” for a “different colour hair clip”. You could ask a question – “I have a question. [Do] you like [the] hair clip?”. Similarly, you could discuss putting on and taking off various make up items and comment on them. You could discuss whether you like the colour or whether a different colour would be better. You might “look [in the] mirror” and comment “OMG! Make up great” or perhaps “uh oh!” and decide to use a “different foundation.”.
