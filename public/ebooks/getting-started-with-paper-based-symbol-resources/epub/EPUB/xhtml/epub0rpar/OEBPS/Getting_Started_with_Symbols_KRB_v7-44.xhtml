Another fun activity can involve creating stories. You could make up a chart, or a series of charts, that someone could use to make up their own stories, or “fill in the gaps” from a range of choices. You could even go on to use symbol based word processing software (e.g. Symwriter from Widgit www.widgit.com) to “publish” the story! Alternatively, you could just write it out on a piece of paper and re-read it together.
