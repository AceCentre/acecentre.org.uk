Chapter 4 – Ideas and Resources for Getting Going

Bubbles

Bubbles are popular with lots of people. Here is a simple chart that you could use to point to whilst playing together. You will notice that some of the symbols are repeated from the Core 8 chart. The repeated symbols are kept in the same place to keep things as consistent as possible.

Even with just eight symbols, you can find a lot to say. You could say that you “like [the] bubbles”, agree to “blow” or “blow more”. You could say “look [at the] bubbles”. You could notice that the bubble has gone “pop” and comment “uh oh!”. You could talk about continuing the activity – “more” or say that the mixture is “finished” and it’s time to “stop”.
