Arts and Crafts

Arts and crafts can be a great way of realising the power of communication.

A lot of fun can be had with some simple colour symbols. For example, in a colouring activity, someone could direct you to colour in a picture for them by telling you which colour to use for each element. Siblings or peers can also take it in turns to tell each other what colour to use.
