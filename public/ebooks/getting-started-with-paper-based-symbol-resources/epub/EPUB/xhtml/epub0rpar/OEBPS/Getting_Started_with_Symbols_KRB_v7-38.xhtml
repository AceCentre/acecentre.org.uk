Medical Kit Chart

Doctors and nurses can be a great opportunity to bring in a communication chart. If you have a pretend medical kit, the chart can be used to indicate where to stick plasters or bandages. They can instruct you to take medicine – yuck! If someone was very ill, you could use a toy phone to call an ambulance, complete with siren noises. You could introduce the concept of “pain” within this play, which could later come in useful when making a communication book page around problems or illness.
