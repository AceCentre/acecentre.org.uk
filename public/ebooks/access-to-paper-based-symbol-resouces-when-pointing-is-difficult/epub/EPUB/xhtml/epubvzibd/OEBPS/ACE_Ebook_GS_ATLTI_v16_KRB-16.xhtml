Occasionally, as discussed with pick up and show, someone will find it easier to grasp a symbol than to point to it. However, for most, the process of picking up the symbol takes more time than simply pointing to it. This can make the communication process slower, particularly if someone is combining symbols on the sentence strip and then returning them to the book.

Careful thought should be given to the introduction of an exchange system. For some, the process of exchange is essential to their becoming a successful communicator. For others, a different paper-based system may be more effective.
