Chapter 6 - Coded Access

Angie uses coded access to select symbols in her PODD communication book

Angie has a PODD communication book that is coded. Each double page spread contains four quadrants of twenty-five symbols, giving a total of one hundred symbols per double page spread. Using the chart on her lap, Angie can direct her communication partner to her target symbol by specifying the quadrant it is in, and then its column and row position. This system gives her access to a substantial symbol vocabulary.
