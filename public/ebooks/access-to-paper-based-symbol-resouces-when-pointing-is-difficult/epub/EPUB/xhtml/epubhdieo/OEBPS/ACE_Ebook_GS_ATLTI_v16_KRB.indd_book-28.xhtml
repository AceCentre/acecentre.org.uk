Chapter 4 - Eye Pointing

Section 1 - E-tran Frames and Alternatives

One alternative to an E-tran frame is to use a piece of A3 or A4 laminate run through the laminator empty. In this instance, gloss laminate works a bit better than matt as it is transparent once laminated.

Symbols can either be laminated inside the pouch, or blu-tacked onto the laminate sheet. The advantage of laminating them inside the pouch is that it ensures the symbol’s location is consistent each time the eye pointing chart is used.

Using laminate as an alternative to an E-tran frame
