Another alternative to colour is to use different border styles like dashes, dots, hatching etc. Rather than encoding using numerals, you could also try using shapes like triangles, squares, circles, hearts etc..

Common ways of setting up encoding include, but are not limited to, four or six groups of two symbols, four or six groups of four symbols, six groups of six symbols, and eight groups of eight symbols on a page.
