Tiago uses colour encoding with his alphabet chart

This video shows Tiago eye pointing to a colour encoded alphabet chart. He first looks at the block that contains his target letter. He then clarifies which letter he would like by looking at the coloured dot that matches the letter colour. Here encoding is used with an alphabet chart, but the principles are the same whether it is letters or symbols that are encoded. Note that as it is filmed from the perspective of the communication partner, we see the mirror image of the alphabet chart that Tiago is eye pointing to.
