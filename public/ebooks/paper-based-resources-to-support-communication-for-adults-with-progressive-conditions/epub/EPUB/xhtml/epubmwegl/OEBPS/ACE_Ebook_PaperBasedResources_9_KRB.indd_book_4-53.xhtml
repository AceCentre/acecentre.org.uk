When we communicate, there is a core set of words that we use time and time again. There are various lists of such commonly used or high frequency words in english, e.g. here on Wikipedia. You might choose to include some of these words on your alphabet chart. They reduce the number of words you need to spell out, but make the chart larger and busier to look at.

These charts by Amy and pALS incorporate high frequency words. They are designed to be accessed directly by pointing, but modified versions could be used with listener mediated scanning:
