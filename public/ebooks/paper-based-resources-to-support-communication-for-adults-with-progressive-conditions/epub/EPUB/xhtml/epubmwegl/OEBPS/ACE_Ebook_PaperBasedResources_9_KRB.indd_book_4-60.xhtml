Communication Books that can be Accessed by Listener Mediated ScanningJust as alphabet charts can be accessed by listener mediated scanning, so too can communication books. The communication partner offers you each message in turn by pointing to the message and / or speaking it aloud. You then indicate when they point to and / or say your target message.

The next pictures illustrate a communication book that is designed to be accessed by listener mediated scanning. The top page in this example contains the categories Quick / urgent phrases, Spelling, Chat, Care needs, Positions, People, Equipment, Feelings and the Dog. The template can be downloaded from Ace Centre’s website – acecentre.org.uk.
