If writing is possible, simply making sure that you always have a notebook and pen to hand can be a powerful communication tool. To help keep the notebook and pen together, you may want to attach the pen to the spine of the notebook.

Another option is to use a whiteboard / wipeclean board (or similar) with a dry erase pen or an LCD writing tablet / doodle pad where you can easily erase what you have written. There are a wide variety of products available in shops and online, and although some are a bit childlike in appearance, you will find others that are appropriate for use by an adult. Such tools can help with privacy as the message can be erased before another is communicated.

It is worth keeping a note of messages or phrases that you are writing repeatedly, as these can be delivered more efficiently. See Chapter 10 for more information.
