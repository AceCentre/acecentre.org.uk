You could also create your own eye pointing communication book and display it in an easel file / presentation portfolio like in this example. Easel files tend to be available from office stationery retailers. Alternatively, a customised eye pointing book could be placed on a recipe book or tablet stand. This lifts the pages making it easier for the communication partner to follow your eye pointing.

The first page of the book is a Home Page, which gives access to lots of topic pages of your choosing. The book also contains a spelling page that can be accessed from the Home Page and every topic page.
