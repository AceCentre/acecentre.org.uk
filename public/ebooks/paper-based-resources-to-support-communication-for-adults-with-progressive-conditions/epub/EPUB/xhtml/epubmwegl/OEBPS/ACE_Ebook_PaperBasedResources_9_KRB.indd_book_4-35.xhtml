The arrows show the eye movements used to select letters on the eye pointing chart. With an unfamiliar communication partner, these may need to be exaggerated. It may be possible to use more subtle movements with a familiar communication partner.

If the chart is very small, try and keep your eye movements precise, perhaps exaggerating them when communicating with less familiar communication partners.

The MegabeeThe Megabee is effectively an electronic E-tran frame, and is the tool chosen by Lorraine to complement her electronic communication aid. It incorporates a display which allows the communication partner to keep track of what has been selected without using pen and paper / similar. The display can be read by both the communication partner and the person communicating using eye pointing. However, there is no speech output.
