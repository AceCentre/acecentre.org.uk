Provide Pen and PaperProviding a communication partner with a pen and paper can help to lift the pressure, as they can write down the letters that you are selecting rather than trying to remember them. Alternatively, a whiteboard with dry erase pen or LCD writing tablet can work well. If there is no pen or paper to hand, people often have a smartphone on them and could be encouraged to use its note facility.

Simplify Your Sentences or Spelling (if you feel comfortable doing so)This is entirely a matter of personal preference, but some people choose to use key words or short sentences to improve the efficiency of their communication. If you are comfortable using them, simplified spellings can also speed things along e.g. ‘u’ for ‘you’ or ‘wen’ for ‘when’. This is a strategy used by Lorraine.
