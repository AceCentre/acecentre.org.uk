Predict What You Might be Communicating (But only if you allow it!)If the context is known, a familiar communication partner may be able to predict a word or even sentence you are communicating after only a couple of letters. It would be worth reflecting on how you feel about this. Some regard this as beneficial as it can speed up communication, while others find it irritating. It is also worth noting that some communication partners are better at it than others. There is no right or wrong approach. The key thing is to be clear about what you want your communication partner to do. This could be documented alongside any other instructions or even written on the tool itself if it is a blanket rule. If you do allow your communication partner to attempt to predict what you are saying, make sure that they confirm with you that they have guessed correctly before moving on. You may also want to agree a maximum number of guesses before you return to spelling out the word or sentence.
