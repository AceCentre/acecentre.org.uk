An E-tran frame is designed to be held by the communication partner, although a well-positioned stand can be used. The E-tran frame is held so that you can make eye contact through the central window. The communication partner can see where on the frame you are looking and therefore work out what letter you are selecting.

The letters tend to be laid out in alphabetical order for use by the person communicating with their eyes with the communication partner seeing the mirror image.
