Coded charts can be set up and accessed in lots of different ways.

If pointing to the codes became difficult, it is possible to present the codes on an eye pointing chart or access them using listener mediated scanning. Alternatively, you might want to switch to a system that has been designed with eye pointing or listener mediated scanning in mind.

As always, the provision of instructions and training for communication partners will be important. You might also consider having a more straightforward communication chart available for quick communication with unfamiliar communication partners.
