Pointing ToolsPointing tools are designed to help people with disabilities type on a computer keyboard with greater accuracy. However, they may also help you to point more accurately to letters on an alphabet chart. They come in many different shapes and sizes and are known by numerous names, including typing aid, keyboard aid, accessible styli, touch enabling device, dibber, universal cuff and more. A range of pointing tools are available from
