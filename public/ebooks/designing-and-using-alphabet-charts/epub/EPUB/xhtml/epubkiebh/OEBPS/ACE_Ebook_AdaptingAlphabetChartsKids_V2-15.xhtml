Pointing tools come in many different shapes and sizes and are known by numerous names, including typing aid, keyboard aid, accessible styli, touch enabling device, dibber, universal cuff and more. A range of pointing tools are available from dadindashed.com (although there are other suppliers!). Some people find that simply wrapping an elastic band around the top of a ball point pen or a standard stylus works well.

Not everyone finds them comfortable to wear or hold, and some find it inconvenient to have another piece of equipment involved in communicating.

Note that there are pointing tools that can be used with other parts of the body e.g. a head pointer.

An alternative pointing tool is an LED pointer. LED pointers are designed to help presenters highlight key points when delivering presentations and are therefore easy to source. They can be used to select letters on an alphabet chart using head movement, but they can also be held in the hand.
