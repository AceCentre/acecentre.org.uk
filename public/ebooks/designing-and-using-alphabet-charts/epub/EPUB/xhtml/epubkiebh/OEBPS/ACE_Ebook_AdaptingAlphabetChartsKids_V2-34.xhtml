To use the chart, the communication partner would offer the first letter in each row. Once the individual indicates ‘yes’ to the row that contains their letter, the communication partner then offers each letter along that row until the individual makes their selection. For example, to communicate the letter ‘S’, the communication partner would first offer ‘space’ then ‘T’ then ‘N’. Once they had offered ‘N’, the individual would indicate ‘yes’ as ‘N’ is at the start of the row that contains the letter ‘S’. The communication partner would then offer ‘N’ then ‘S’ and the individual would indicate ‘yes’.
