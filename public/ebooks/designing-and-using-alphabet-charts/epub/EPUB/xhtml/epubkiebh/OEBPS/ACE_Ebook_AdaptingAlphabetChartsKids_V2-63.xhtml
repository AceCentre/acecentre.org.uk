﻿An example of an A4 communication book based around spelling, core words and phrases

In the above example, the alphabet is secured to the front cover and a page containing numbers, time words and special events is secured to the inside cover. There are then pages of phrases and words sorted by the personalised categories: Quick / Urgent messages, Chat (general / chat with husband / chat with grandchildren), Feelings, People, Care needs, Health issues, Equipment, Chores, Shopping list items and the Cat.

The next set of pictures illustrates some pages from a different style of communication book that is designed to be accessed by pointing. It is a larger A4 sized book that uses a mixture of spelling, core words and phrases. The template can be downloaded from acecentre.org.uk.
