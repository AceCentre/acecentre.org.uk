Ace Centre has developed a range of encoded eye pointing charts that can be downloaded free of charge - https://acecentre.org.uk/product-category/alphabet-charts/alphabet-eye-point/. Some require access to a laminator to create them, but others can be printed and used without laminating.

Jason Becker, who has had ALS for thirty years, has developed an eye pointing chart called VocalEyes based on encoding. You can see him using Vocal Eyes and download the chart for free here: http://jasonbeckerarchive.com/eye_communication.html
