﻿Anjali is sixteen years old. She has cerebral palsy, autism and epilepsy. She is able to verbalise a range of sounds, but cannot speak any recognisable words. Although speech is difficult, Anjali’s literacy skills are very good. She uses an alphabetically organised alphabet chart to support her communication.

Anjali uses her alphabet chart with everyone!!! She is very proficient at pointing to letters and loves to ask questions such as, “which supermarket do you shop at?”

Anjali is also exploring whether a voice output communication aid might be useful to her, alongside her alphabet chart.
